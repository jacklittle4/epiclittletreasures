About the shop

A cheerful little handmade shop with lots of heart.

Epic Little Treasures is built around hand stitched, hand dyed, and handmade pieces. Each journal, paper set, textile pack, and keepsake has its own color, texture, and tiny finishing details.

The maker

Handmade, one of a kind, and made with a lot of heart.

Everything in the shop is hand stitched, hand dyed, and handmade by Stephanie, so no two pieces are quite alike. The little details are the whole point: paper grain, frayed fabric, lace, ribbon, dye marks, and pressed-flower touches.
