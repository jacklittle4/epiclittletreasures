Helpful answers

A few simple answers before you buy or send a message.

Handmade work is personal, and ordering should feel easy. These answers keep the basics clear.

How do the junk journal bundles work?

Bundle levels are based on pulls: 10 pulls for $35, 15 pulls for $45, 20 pulls for $55, and 25 pulls for $65. Listed bundle prices include shipping.

How do I buy something?

Choose an item on the shop page, then tap Buy Now. Fixed-price pieces can be paid through Cash App or Venmo. Use the contact form for custom work, shipping details, or questions.

Are the prices final?

Listed prices are friendly starting prices. Final price can change with size, materials, detail, quantity, and custom requests.

Can I request a custom piece?

Yes. Custom journals, paper sets, and gift bundles can start with a simple idea, color palette, occasion, budget, and deadline.

Are pieces one of a kind?

Yes. The shop describes everything as hand stitched, hand dyed, handmade, and one of a kind, so materials and finishing details vary.

Where do I see the newest work?

TikTok is the best place for live sales and current work. Facebook, email, and the contact form are good for follow-up questions.

What should I include in my order message?

Include item name, quantity, color preferences, occasion, budget comfort, deadline, pickup or shipping needs, and whether it is a gift.

Does the site take payments?

Yes. Fixed-price products link to Cash App and Venmo for quick payment. A full card cart would require a separate checkout service later.

Ready?

Buy a listed piece or send a quick note. It does not need to be fancy.
