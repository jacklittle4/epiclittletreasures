Shop terms

Clear expectations for handmade purchases.

These terms are simple notes for a small handmade shop.

Availability

One-of-a-kind pieces can sell quickly through TikTok lives. Ask first if you are unsure an item is still available.

Payment

Fixed-price pieces can be paid through Cash App or Venmo. Include the item name in the payment note.

Shipping and pickup

Send shipping or pickup details after payment. Timing depends on the item, current workload, and custom needs.

Returns

Handmade one-of-a-kind items are generally final sale unless there is a problem with the order that needs follow-up.
